About me

Hello, it's a pleasure to have you visit my personal homepage 👋!

I am a third-year undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering at the School of Computer and Information Science and the School of Software at Southwest University. My research interests include Robotics, Embedded Development, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision (Detection, Segmentation, Image/Video Processing).

Currently, I am engaged in the development of robots related to the DJI RoboMaster competition at the GKD Robotics Innovation Laboratory of Southwest University. Additionally, I am involved in embedded software development activities with the Southwest University Computer Art Development Team. Furthermore, I conduct research on computer vision and machine learning, particularly in the areas of image processing and image translation, at the Computer Vision and Cross-Media Laboratory (CVCM Lab) of Southwest University.

Now, I am looking for PhD opportunities for Autumn 2024 admission. If you are interested in my research or want to offer me an opportunity, thank you and welcome to contact me!
